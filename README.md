## Overview

Selecting good companies is only one part of successful investing.

The quality of the overall portfolio is equally important.

Even a portfolio containing excellent businesses can produce poor investment outcomes if it is overly concentrated, heavily exposed to a single sector or lacks appropriate diversification.

This project extends the work started in my **AI Equity Research Agent**.

While the Equity Research Agent evaluates individual companies, the AI Portfolio Analysis Agent evaluates how those companies work together as an investment portfolio.

The workflow transforms a list of portfolio holdings into a structured portfolio review, helping investors understand strengths, weaknesses and potential risks before making allocation decisions.

Rather than replacing investment judgement, the objective is to provide a disciplined framework that supports better portfolio construction.

# The Problem

Many investors spend significant time researching individual companies but very little time evaluating the portfolio itself.

Questions such as:

- Is my portfolio too concentrated?
- Am I taking unnecessary sector risk?
- Do my holdings complement each other?
- Am I genuinely diversified?
- What are the biggest risks across the portfolio?

often remain unanswered.

Most available tools focus on performance tracking rather than portfolio quality.

This project aims to bridge that gap.

---

# The Solution

The AI Portfolio Analysis Agent evaluates a portfolio using a structured framework rather than relying on isolated observations.

The workflow analyses:

- Portfolio allocation
- Diversification
- Concentration risk
- Sector exposure
- Portfolio quality
- Business quality of holdings
- Valuation considerations
- Risk assessment
- Scenario analysis

The objective is not to recommend buying or selling securities automatically.

Instead, it provides an evidence-based review that helps investors understand whether their portfolio aligns with their investment objectives and risk tolerance.
