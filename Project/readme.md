# Project Documentation

## Project Overview

The AI Portfolio Analysis Agent is an AI-assisted workflow designed to evaluate an investment portfolio through a structured and repeatable analytical framework.

Rather than analysing individual companies, the workflow focuses on the portfolio as a whole—assessing diversification, concentration risk, sector exposure, portfolio quality, valuation, expected risk and overall portfolio construction.

The objective is not to recommend what to buy or sell automatically.

Instead, the workflow provides a structured assessment that helps investors understand the strengths, weaknesses and potential risks within their portfolio before making investment decisions.

## Why I Built This

After analysing individual companies, I realised that selecting good stocks is only one part of successful investing.

Portfolio construction is equally important.

A portfolio containing excellent businesses can still perform poorly if it is overly concentrated, heavily exposed to one sector or lacks diversification across industries, market capitalisations or investment themes.

Most retail investors evaluate stocks individually but rarely evaluate the portfolio itself.

This project aims to solve that problem by using AI to review an entire portfolio through a consistent investment framework.

## Project Objectives

The workflow has four primary objectives.

### Evaluate Portfolio Quality

Assess the overall quality of businesses held within the portfolio rather than viewing each investment in isolation.

### Identify Portfolio Risks

Highlight concentration risk, sector exposure, geographical exposure and dependency on a small number of holdings.

### Assess Diversification

Evaluate whether diversification genuinely reduces risk or simply increases the number of holdings without improving portfolio quality.

### Support Better Decision Making

Transform portfolio information into practical insights that help investors improve allocation decisions while remaining aligned with their investment objectives.
